INTRODUCTION

I received my Ph.D. degree in mechatronic engineering from Huazhong university of science and technology (HUST). Previously, I received my Bachelor's and Master's degree in Marine Engineering from HUST, under the supervision of Professor Guohua Xu.

My research interests focus on the design, modeling, and control of wearable robots, aiming to restore the motion capability for stroke patients and enhance the load carriage capability for healthy people.
